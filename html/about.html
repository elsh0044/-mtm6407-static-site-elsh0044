About Me

I’m Ali El Sharkawy, a passionate content creator with a strong foundation in digital media and storytelling. I recently completed my Interactive Media and Design diploma at Algonquin College, where I developed both my technical and creative skills.

My creative journey started with photography, then evolved into motion design and video editing. I’m most energized when I’m combining audio, motion, and visuals to tell human stories — whether it’s through a short film, music video, or brand content.

Software I Use

  • Adobe Premiere Pro — Video editing & color correction
  • Adobe After Effects — Motion graphics, title animation, transitions
  • Adobe Photoshop & Lightroom — Image editing & retouching
  • Figma — Interface and layout mockups
  • Camera Gear — DSLR, gimbals, lighting kits, green screen setups

What I Bring

I’m a fast learner, visual problem solver, and collaborator. Whether I’m shooting a music video or building motion graphics for a client website, I work with intention and creativity — always aiming to make the final product feel alive.
