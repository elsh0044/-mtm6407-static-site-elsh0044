Hi, I'm Ali El Sharkawy

I'm a digital creator based in Ottawa, specializing in videography, photography, and motion graphics. As a graduate of Algonquin College's Interactive Media and Design program, I bring visuals to life through powerful storytelling, emotion, and design.

I use tools like Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, and professional camera gear to create dynamic content for brands, artists, and individuals. Whether it’s a promo video, a photo shoot, or an animated explainer, I bring technical skill and creative vision to every project.

Explore my portfolio, learn more about my process, or reach out to collaborate. I’m always excited to work on new projects that challenge me to push visual boundaries.
